Eşim Sarah Urist Green’e, anagramla:

 

Her great Russian

Grin has treasure...

A great risen rush.

She is a rutranger;

Anguish arrester;

Sister; haranguer,

Treasuresharing,

Heartreassuring

Signature Sharer

Easing rare hurts.1
